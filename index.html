San Francisco Salary Distribution by Gender, 2014

Hope Ashcraft and Tarum Fraz

Purpose: Depict the salary distribution by county, gender, and industry in San Francisco. Determine low and high income areas and how they are affected by different types of people. This graph is useful for anyone who is curious about the cost of living in San Francisco, and how it is affected by different groups of people.
